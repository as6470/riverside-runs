running with the wind

they always say to run like the wind
but what about running with the wind?
feeling it flow through your veins
becoming a part of you
until you can't tell yourself apart
from your surroundings

who needs a sidebar
when you can make a sidenote
i have no notes for you
you're good
go on
read to your heart's desire
